About us

Electro Plumbing Solutions Limited was established in 2013 and formally incorporated in 2016. Our company is dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions for water and electrical infrastructure. Our core competencies include drilling boreholes, installing electrical and solar pumps, implementing solar water heaters, constructing and outfitting swimming pools, maintaining boreholes and swimming pools, and offering equipment sales. We are proud to have a team of qualified and forward-thinking professionals who possess the necessary skill and experience to exceed clients' expectations in addressing contemporary water-related challenges.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower our customers with access to clean and reliable sources of water and energy through the implementation of cutting-edge and advanced technologies.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the undisputed leader in the provision of water and energy solutions throughout Africa, driving progress and sustainability through our commitment to innovation and excellence.

Core Values

To revolutionize the water and electrical industry by delivering innovative and efficient solutions that exceed customer expectations and improve the overall quality of life through reliable and sustainable water and electrical installations.
